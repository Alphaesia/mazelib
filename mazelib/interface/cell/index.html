Reading and manipulating mazes at a high level.

Mazes are fundamentally a collection of cells. These collections are stored in MazeBuffers.

While a collection of cells constitutes a maze, they need to be interpreted before any useful analysis can be done. This is the purpose of the CellManager - to hold the necessary information for interpreting cells.

Recommended Reading

  1. crate::interface’s section on cells — a detailed introduction to cells and what they are.
  2. ConnectionType — the ways that points can be connected.
  3. CellManager — the unifying glue and centre of the maze model.
