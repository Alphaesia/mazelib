Simple sugar for MazeRendererNonSeekings.

Lets you elide constructing renderers with parameterless constructors (specifically, renderers that implement Default).

BoxSpaceTextMazeRenderer::new().render(&mut maze, &mut output)

becomes

BoxSpaceTextMazeRenderer::render(&mut maze, &mut output)
