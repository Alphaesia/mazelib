Expand description

Simple sugar for MazeGenerators.

Lets you elide constructing generators with parameterless constructors (specifically, generators that implement Default).

HuntAndKillGenerator::new().generate(&mut maze);

becomes

HuntAndKillGenerator::generate(&mut maze);
